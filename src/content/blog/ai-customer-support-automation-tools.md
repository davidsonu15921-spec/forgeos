## Understanding AI Customer Support
### What is AI Customer Support?
AI customer support uses machine learning algorithms to automate support tasks, such as answering frequent questions and routing complex issues to human agents.

### Benefits of AI Customer Support
By automating routine tasks, AI customer support saves you time and money. It also enables your human agents to focus on high-value tasks, improving overall customer experience and reducing support costs.

## Measuring ROI of AI Customer Support
### Understanding ROI
Measuring the return on investment (ROI) of AI customer support is crucial to understanding its value. ROI calculations help you determine whether the tool is generating revenue and reducing costs.

### Calculating ROI
To calculate ROI, use the following formula: (Gain - Cost) / Cost. This will give you a clear picture of the tool's financial impact on your business.

## Hybrid AI-Human Customer Support
### Introduction to Hybrid Support
Hybrid AI-human customer support combines the benefits of AI automation with the empathy and problem-solving skills of human agents. This approach provides the best possible customer experience.

### Implementing Hybrid Support
To implement hybrid support, set up a system that uses AI for routine tasks and routes complex issues to human agents. This ensures that customers receive timely and effective support.
